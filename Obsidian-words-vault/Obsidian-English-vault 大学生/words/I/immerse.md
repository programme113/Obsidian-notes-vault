- To break out , you need to totally immerse yourself in the game .
	- 要想成功逃脱，你需要完全沉浸在游戏中。
- Don 't immerse yourself in the infliction too long .
	- 不要长时间沉浸在痛苦经历中。
- She immersed herself in her work .
	- 她埋头工作。
