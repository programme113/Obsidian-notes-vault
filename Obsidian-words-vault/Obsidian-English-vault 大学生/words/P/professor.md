# 词义
- 英：/prəˈfesə(r)/； 美：/prəˈfesər/
- #n 教授；(大学的)讲师，教员
# 例句
- He was made professor at the age of 40 .
	- 他40岁时成为教授。
- Professor Wilson was invited to speak about the results of his research .
	- 威尔逊教授获邀就自己的研究成果发言。
- The evidence suggests , pace Professor Jones , that … .
	- 请琼斯教授原谅，证据表明…
