# 例句
- Eventually , the water will permeate through the surrounding concrete .
	- 最终，水会渗透进周围的混凝土中。
- Water will easily permeate a cotton dress .
	- 水很容易渗透棉布衣服。
- Dissatisfaction among the managers soon permeated down to members of the workforce .
	- 管理人员的不满情绪很快传染给了全体职工。
