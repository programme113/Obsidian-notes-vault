# 词义
- 英：/ˈpʌnɪʃmənt/； 美：/ˈpʌnɪʃmənt/
- #n 惩罚；处罚；刑罚；虐待；粗暴对待
# 例句
- We discussed what punishment should be given to the boys .
	- 我们讨论了该如何惩罚这些男孩。
- The prison service has the twin goals of punishment and rehabilitation .
	- 监狱有惩罚和改造双重目的。
- He was sent to his room as a punishment .
	- 他被罚回到他的房间。
