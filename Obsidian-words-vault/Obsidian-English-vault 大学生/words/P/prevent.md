# 词义
- 英：/prɪˈvent/； 美：/prɪˈvent/
- #vt 防止；阻止；阻碍；阻挠
# 例句
- They took active steps to prevent the spread of the disease .
	- 他们采取积极措施，防止疾病蔓延。
- We are taking steps to prevent pollution .
	- 我们正在采取措施防止污染。
- Snow has prevented communication with the outside world for three days .
	- 大雪使得与外界的通信联系中断了三天。
