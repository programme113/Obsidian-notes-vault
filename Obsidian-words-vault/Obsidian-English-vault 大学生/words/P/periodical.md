- 英：/ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkl/； 美：/ˌpɪriˈɑːdɪkl/

- The quarterly magazine is a periodical published every three months .

	- 期刊自动化管理上的应用与思考
- The walls would be lined with books and periodicals .
