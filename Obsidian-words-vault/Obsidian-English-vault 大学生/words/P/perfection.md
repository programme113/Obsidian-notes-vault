# 词义
- 英：/pəˈfekʃn/； 美：/pərˈfekʃn/
- #n 完美；完善；圆满；最后加工
# 例句
- My idea of physical perfection is to be very slender .
	- 在我看来，要拥有完美身材就要非常苗条。
- He is rarely satisfied with anything less than perfection itself .
	- 他一般只对那些绝对完美的事情感到满意。
- They have been working on the perfection of the new model .
	- 他们一直在努力完善新型号。
# 形态
- #word_pl perfections
