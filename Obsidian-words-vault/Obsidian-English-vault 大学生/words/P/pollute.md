# 词义
- 英：/pəˈluːt/； 美：/pəˈluːt/
- #vt 污染；弄脏
# 例句
- These gases pollute the atmosphere of towns and cities .
	- 这些气体污染了城镇的空气。
- Mining would pollute the lake and denude the forest
	- 采矿会污染湖水，毁掉森林。
- The river has been polluted with toxic waste from local factories .
	- 当地工厂排放的有毒废弃物污染了这条河。
