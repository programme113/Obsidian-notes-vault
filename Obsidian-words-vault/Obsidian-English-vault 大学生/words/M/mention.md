# 词义
- 英：/ˈmenʃn/； 美：/ˈmenʃn/
- #vt 提到；说到；写到
- #n 提及；说起；写上一笔
# 例句
- Come to think of it , he did mention seeing you .
	- 想起来了，他确实提到看见过你。
- He failed to mention that he was the one who started the fight .
	- 他没提到是他先动手打人的。
- The advantages of this system are too numerous to mention .
	- 这套系统的好处不胜枚举。
