# 词义
- 英：/ˈmɜːdʒə(r)/； 美：/ˈmɜːrdʒər/
- #n 合并；(机构或企业的)归并
# 例句
- The merger is by no means a done deal yet .
	- 合并之事远未成定局。
- Two hundred people lost their jobs as a direct consequence of the merger .
	- 合并一事直接导致二百人失去了工作。
- Share prices took a sharp tumble following news of the merger .
	- 合并消息传出，股价随即暴跌。
