# 词义
- 英：/rɪˈzent/； 美：/rɪˈzent/
- #vt 怨恨；愤恨；愤愤不平；感到气愤
# 例句
- It is only natural that he should resent you
	- 他怨恨你是很自然的事。
- I resent the obloquy that you are casting upon my reputation .
	- 我怨恨你对我的名誉横加诽谤。
- They resent foreign interference in the internal affairs of their country .
	- 他们憎恶对他们国家内政的外来干涉。
