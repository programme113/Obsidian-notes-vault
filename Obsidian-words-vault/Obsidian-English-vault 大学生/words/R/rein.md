- #v 控制；驾驭；勒住
# 例句
- A rein to direct the horse 's head left or right .
	- 控制马头向左或向右的缰绳。
- We 'd better give the horse a free rein .
	- 我们最好放松缰绳任凭马奔跑。
- It was time to hand over the reins of power .
	- 是该让权的时候了。
