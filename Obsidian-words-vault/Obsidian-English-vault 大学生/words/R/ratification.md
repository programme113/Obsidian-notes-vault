# 例句
- The EU will now complete ratification of the treaty by June 1 .
	- 欧盟将于6月1日前完成条约的批准。
- The treaty awaits ratification .
	- 这项条约等待着批准。
- The second chapter expounds the approval and ratification of the reorganization project .
	- 第二章论述了公司重整计划的通过与批准。
