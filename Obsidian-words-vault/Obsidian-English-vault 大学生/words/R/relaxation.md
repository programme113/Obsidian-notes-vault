- Diet and relaxation are two important keys to good health .

- Everyone needs some free time for rest and relaxation
	- 每个人都需要一些休息和放松的自由时间。
- Fishing is his favourite relaxation .
	- 钓鱼是他最喜欢的消遣活动。
