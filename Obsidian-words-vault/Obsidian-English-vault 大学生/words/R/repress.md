# 词义
- 英：/rɪˈpres/； 美：/rɪˈpres/
- #vt 镇压；抑制；压制；压抑；克制
# 例句
- People who repress their emotions risk having nightmares
	- 压抑情感的人容易做噩梦。
- Certain needs he can no longer repress .
	- 他已经不能再压抑。
- He burst in , making no effort to repress his fury .
	- 他冲了进来，毫不掩饰自己的愤怒。
