# 词义
- 英：/ˈrɪski/； 美：/ˈrɪski/

- Heavy reliance on one client is risky when you are building up a business .
	- 创业时过分依赖某一个客户是有风险的。
- Horse breeding is indeed a risky enterprise .
	- 养马确实是个有风险的营生。
- It 's far too risky to generalize from one set of results .
	- 仅根据一组结果进行概括是十分不可靠的。
