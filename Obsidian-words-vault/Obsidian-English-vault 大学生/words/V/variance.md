# 例句
- In statistical language , this estimate is called the between-column variance .
	- 在统计学中这个估计值叫组间方差。

	- 基于类别方差的三维医学图像分割新方法
- Many of his statements were at variance with the facts
	- 他的说法有很多和事实不符。
