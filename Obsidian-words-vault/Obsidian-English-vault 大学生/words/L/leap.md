# 词义
- 英：/liːp/； 美：/liːp/

- #n 跳跃；跳高；激增；剧增；跳越；骤变
# 例句
- His heart gave a sudden leap when he saw her .
	- 他看见她时，心猛地一跳。
- He who would leap high must take a long run .
	- 要跳得高必得要一个长跑。
- Few people successfully make the leap from television to the movies .
	- 从电视业转向电影业很少有人成功。
