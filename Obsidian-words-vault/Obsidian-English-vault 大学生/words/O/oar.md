# 词义
- 英：/ɔː(r)/； 美：/ɔːr/
- #n 桨；船桨
- #vi 划（行）
# 例句
- The blade of the oar had entangled itself with something in the water .
	- 船桨的桨叶和水里的什么东西缠住了。
- He got hold of the oar and was pulled out of the water .
	- 他抓住船桨而被拉出水面。
- He pulled as hard as he could on the oars .
	- 他拼命地划桨。
