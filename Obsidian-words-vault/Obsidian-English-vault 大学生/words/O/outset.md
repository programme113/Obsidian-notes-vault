# 词义
- 英：/ˈaʊtset/； 美：/ˈaʊtset/
- #n 开始；开端 ；起始
# 例句
- A little forward planning at the outset can save you a lot of expense .
	- 一开始就为未来作点打算能节约很多开支。
- I made it clear right from the outset that I disapproved .
	- 从一开始我就明确地说我不赞成。
- Decide at the outset what kind of learning programme you want to follow
	- 开始时就选定自己的学习方案。
