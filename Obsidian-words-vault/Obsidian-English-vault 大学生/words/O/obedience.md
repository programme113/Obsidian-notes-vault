# 词义
- 英：/əˈbiːdiəns/； 美：/oʊˈbidiəns/
- #n 服从；顺从；遵从
# 例句
- Isabella had been taught unquestioning obedience
	- 伊莎贝拉被要求无条件服从。
- You will have to beat the old man into obedience .
	- 你得强迫那老头服从。
- Obedience by women towards men is a remnant of religious teaching .
	- 女人对男人的顺从是宗教教义的残迹。
