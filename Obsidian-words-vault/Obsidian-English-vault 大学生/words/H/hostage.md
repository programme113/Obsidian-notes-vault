# 词义
- 英：/ˈhɒstɪdʒ/； 美：/ˈhɑːstɪdʒ/
- #n 人质
# 例句
- Three children were taken hostage during the bank robbery .
	- 在银行抢劫案中有三名儿童被扣为人质。
- He was held hostage for almost a year .
	- 他被扣为人质几近一年。
- The release of the hostages could not be achieved without the use of force .
	- 不使用武力不可能使人质获释。
