# 例句
- It is known for its handicraft products .
	- 这地方因出产手工艺品而闻名。
- The delicate handicraft works will not bear rough handling .
	- 精致的手工艺品经不起粗鲁的摆弄。
- Her hobbies are music , reading and handicraft .
	- 她的爱好是音乐、读书和手工。
