# 词义

# 例句
- The factory closed its doors for the last time in 2002 .
	- 这家工厂最后于2002年关闭。
- Without education , these children will end up as factory fodder .
	- 不受教育，这些孩子将来只能到工厂干活。
- We have had problems with the supply of raw materials to the factory .
	- 我们在工厂的原料供应方面遇到过问题。
