# 词义
- 英：/bɪˈwɪldə(r)/； 美：/bɪˈwɪldər/
- #vt 使迷惑；使糊涂
# 例句
- He turned around , with a bewildered look on his face .
	- 他转过身来，满脸困惑。
- There is a bewildering variety of software available .
	- 各种可供挑选的软件使人目不暇接。
- She was totally bewildered by his sudden change of mood .
	- 他的情绪突变搞得她全然不知所措。
