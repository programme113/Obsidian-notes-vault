# 例句
- The air was so cold we could hardly breathe .

- They inserted a tube in his mouth to help him breathe .
	- 他们在他嘴里插了根导管帮助他呼吸。
- Breathing is an automatic function of the body .
	- 呼吸是一种无意识的身体功能。
