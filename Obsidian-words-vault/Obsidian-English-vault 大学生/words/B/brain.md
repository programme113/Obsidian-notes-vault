- #vt 猛击…的脑袋致死
# 例句
- Once destroyed , brain cells do not regenerate .
	- 脑细胞一旦遭到破坏，就不能再生。
- There is a small risk of brain damage from the procedure
	- 该手术有导致脑损伤的轻微风险。
- She is best known for her work on the human brain .
	- 她在对人脑的研究方面最为知名。
