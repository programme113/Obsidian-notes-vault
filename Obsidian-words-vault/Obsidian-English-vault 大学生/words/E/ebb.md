# 词义
- 英：/eb/； 美：/eb/
- #n 落潮；退潮
- #vi 落；衰退；退；减退；衰弱
# 例句
- The flood and ebb tides alternates with each other .
	- 涨潮和落潮交替更迭。
- Bottom Sediment Transport in the Flood and Ebb Channels of the Changjiang Estuary
	- 长江口涨、落潮槽底沙输移趋势探讨
- As night fell , our enthusiasm began to ebb away .
	- 我们的热情随着夜晚的降临而渐渐低落下来。
