# 例句
- You can expect to earn round about £ 40,000 a year .
	- 你可望一年赚4万英镑左右。
- If you earn more , you pay more in tax , so it 's all swings and roundabouts .
	- 赚的越多，缴的税也越多，所以有得必有失。
- It 's about time you got a job to earn your keep .
	- 你该找个工作挣自己的生活费了。
