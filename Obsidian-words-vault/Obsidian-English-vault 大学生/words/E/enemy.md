# 词义
- 英：/ˈenəmi/； 美：/ˈenəmi/
- #n 敌人；敌军；敌国；仇人；大敌；敌兵；反对者；危害物
- #adj 敌人的
# 例句
- The city is under enemy control .

- We are now within range of enemy fire .
	- 我们现在处于敌人的火力射程以内。
- After just one day , she had already made an enemy of her manager .
