# 例句
- The president is responsible for the designation of ambassadors .
	- 总统负责对大使的任命。
- The district is under consideration for designation as a conservation area .
	- 正在考虑将这个地区指定为保护区。
- Her official designation is Financial Controller .
	- 她的正式职务是财务总监。
