# 词义
- 英：/dɔː(r)/； 美：/dɔːr/
- #n 门；门口；门边；出入口；栋；门旁
# 例句
- Why oh why do people keep leaving the door open ?
	- 人们到底为什么总敞着门呢？
- For reasons of security the door is always kept locked .
	- 为了保证安全，门总是锁着的。
- The agreement will open the door to increased international trade .
	- 此协议将会提供增加国际贸易的机会。
# 形态
- #word_pl doors
