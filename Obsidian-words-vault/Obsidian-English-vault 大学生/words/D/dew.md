# 词义
- 英：/djuː/； 美：/duː/
- #n 露水；露
- #vt 弄湿
# 例句
- The grass was wet with early morning dew .
	- 清晨的露水使得青草湿漉漉的。
- The dew gathered on the leaves .
	- 露水在叶子上凝结。
- If it were not for the vapour in the air , there would be no dew .
	- 如果空气中没有水气，就没有露水。
