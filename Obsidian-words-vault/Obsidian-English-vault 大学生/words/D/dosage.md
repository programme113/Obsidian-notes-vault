# 词义
- 英：/ˈdəʊsɪdʒ/； 美：/ˈdoʊsɪdʒ/
- #n (通常指药的)剂量
# 例句
- Do not exceed the recommended dosage .
	- 切勿超过规定剂量。
- He had improved so much the doctor had cut his dosage .
	- 他康复很快，医生已减少了用药剂量。
- His doctor increased the dosage but did not change to a different medication
	- 他的医生加大了剂量，不过没有换药。
