# 词义
- 英：/ˈsʌmtaɪmz/； 美：/ˈsʌmtaɪmz/
- #adv 有时；间或
# 例句
- Students sometimes do casual work in the tourist trade .
	- 学生有时做些旅游方面的零工。
- Young children are beginning their formal education sometimes as early as four years old .
	- 幼儿有时早在四岁时就开始接受正规教育。
- I like to be on my own sometimes .
	- 有时候我喜欢一个人待着。
