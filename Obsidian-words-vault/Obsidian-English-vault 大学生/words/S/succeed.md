# 例句
- They should abandon the conventional wisdom and , instead , dare to succeed .
	- 他们应抛弃传统观念，勇于达到目的。
- The thief tried to buy off the policeman , but he did not succeed .
	- 这贼企图贿赂警察，但他未达到目的。
- You will have to work hard if you are to succeed .
	- 要想有所作为，你必须苦干。
