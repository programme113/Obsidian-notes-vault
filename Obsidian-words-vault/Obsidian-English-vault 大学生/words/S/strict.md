# 词义
- 英：/strɪkt/； 美：/strɪkt/
- #adj 严格的(指必须恪守)；严厉的；严密的；恪守教规(或信条等)的
# 例句
- The government has set strict limits on public spending this year .
	- 今年，政府对公共开支规定了严格的限额。
- The EU has set strict limits on levels of pollution .
	- 欧盟对污染程度作了严格的限制。
- They were always very strict with their children .
	- 他们对子女一向十分严格。
# 形态
- #word_est strictest
- #word_er stricter
