# 词义
- 英：/ˈʃɔːtɪdʒ/； 美：/ˈʃɔːrtɪdʒ/
- #n 短缺；不足；缺少
# 例句
- Patients were dying because of an acute shortage of nurses .
	- 因为护理人员严重不足，患者生命垂危。
- There is a shortage of council housing
	- 廉租公房房源不足。
- The country experienced a foreign currency shortage for several months .
	- 这个国家经历了几个月的外汇短缺。
