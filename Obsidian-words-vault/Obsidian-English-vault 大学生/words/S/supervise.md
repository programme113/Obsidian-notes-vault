- The UN pledged to help supervise the clearance of mines .
	- 联合国承诺协助监督扫雷工作。
- I supervise the packing of all mail orders .
	- 我负责监督所有邮购商品的包装过程。
- She supervised the children playing near the pool .
	- 她照料着在水池附近玩的几个孩子。
