# 词义
- 英：/dʒuːn/； 美：/dʒuːn/
- #n 六月
# 例句
- They would have been married forty years come this June .
	- 到今年六月他们结婚就有四十年了。
- The committee will present its final report to Parliament in June .
	- 委员会将于六月向议会提交最后的报告。
- The new sports complex is on target to open in June .
	- 新建的体育中心将在六月份如期开放。
