# 词义
- 英：/ɔːˈɡʌst/； 美：/ɔːˈɡʌst/
- #adj 威严的，庄严的
# 例句
- Being so near the august person of the Empress , he was overcome with awe .
	- 如此靠近威严的女皇，他心中充满了敬畏。
- He was probably the most august figure in the house of lords .
	- 他可能是上议院最威严的人物。
- Germany declared war on France on 1 August 1914 .
	- 德国在1914年8月1日向法国宣战。
