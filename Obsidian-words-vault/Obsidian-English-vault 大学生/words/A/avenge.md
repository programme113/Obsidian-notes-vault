# 词义
- 英：/əˈvendʒ/； 美：/əˈvendʒ/
- #vt 复仇；报(某事)之仇；向(某人)报仇
# 例句
- He vowed to avenge himself .
	- 他发誓要复仇。
- you can be damn well sure we 'll avenge it .
	- 绝对会为之复仇
- She was determined to avenge herself on the man who had betrayed her .
	- 她决心向那个负心男人报仇。
