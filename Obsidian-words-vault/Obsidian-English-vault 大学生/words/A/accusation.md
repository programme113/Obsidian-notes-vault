- He denied the accusation that he had ignored the problems .
	- 他否认别人对他忽视这些问题的指控。

	- 这一指控使她极为痛苦。
- If you 're going to make accusations , you 'd better get your facts right .
	- 你要是打算控告最好把证据弄确凿。
