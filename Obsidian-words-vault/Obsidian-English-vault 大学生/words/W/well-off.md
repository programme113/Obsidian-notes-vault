# 词义
- 英：/ˌwel ˈɒf/； 美：/ˌwel ˈɔːf/
- #adj 小康；富裕的；繁荣昌盛的
# 例句
- A state-owned enterprise is big and well-off .
	- 国营企业家业大。
- I am well-off for winter clothing .
	- 我的冬衣很充裕。
- My parents were hard up during the1930s but now they are well-off .
	- 在三十年代，我的双亲经济拮据，但现在他们很富有。
# 形态
- #word_est best-off
- #word_er better-off
