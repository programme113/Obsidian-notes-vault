# 词义
- 英：/kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/； 美：/koʊˈɑːpəreɪt/
- #vi 合作；配合；协作；协助
# 例句
- Are you going to cooperate or do we have to get physical ?
	- 你是合作呢，还是要我们动手？
- In his letter he indicated to us he was willing to cooperate .
	- 他在信中向我们透露他愿意合作。
- The two groups agreed to cooperate with each other .
	- 这两个组同意相互协作。
