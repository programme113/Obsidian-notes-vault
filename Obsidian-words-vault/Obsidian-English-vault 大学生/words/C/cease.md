# 词义
- 英：/siːs/； 美：/siːs/
- #v 停止；(使)终止；结束
# 例句
- He ordered his men to cease fire .
	- 他命令手下停止射击。
- They voted to cease strike action immediately .
	- 他们投票决定立即停止罢工。
- Welfare payments cease as soon as an individual starts a job .
	- 一旦就业，即停发福利救济。
