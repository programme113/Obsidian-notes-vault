# 词义
- 英：/kənˈspɪrəsi/； 美：/kənˈspɪrəsi/
- #n 阴谋；密谋策划
# 例句
- They were charged with conspiracy to murder .
	- 他们被指控密谋策划谋杀。
- Many people were involved in this conspiracy , but three stand out .
	- 很多人参与了这场阴谋，但有3个人是主谋。
- All three men were charged with conspiracy to defraud .
	- 三人均被控密谋诈骗。
