# 词义
- 英：/keɪdʒ/； 美：/keɪdʒ/
- #n 笼子
- #vt 把(动物)关在笼中
# 例句
- Keep the dog confined in a suitable travelling cage .
	- 把狗关进适于旅行的笼子里。
- Don 't stick your fingers through the bars of the cage .
	- 不要把指头伸进笼子里。
- Many caged birds live longer than their fellows in the wild .
	- 许多笼中鸟比野外同类鸟的寿命长。
