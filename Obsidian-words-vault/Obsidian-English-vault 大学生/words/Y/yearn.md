# 词义
- 英：/jɜːn/； 美：/jɜːrn/
- #vi 渴望；渴求
# 例句
- Is it not in the nature of all human beings to yearn for freedom ?
	- 渴望自由不是人类的天性吗？
- Many young people in the country yearn for city life .
	- 许多农村的年轻人渴望城市生活。
- She had no great yearning to go back .
	- 她并不十分想回去。
